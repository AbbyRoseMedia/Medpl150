Guastella, Abby

My name is Abby Guastella and I'm a 20 year old Hunter College student based in New York City. My work aims to showcase young adults in their 20s throughout New York with various creative outlets and talents. This includes but is not limited to college students with a passion in pursuing a career in dance, acting, drawing, painting, singing and songwriting. I give an inside look at the hard work, dedication and endless practice one undergoes trying to break their way into the New York scene while naviagating college life. Through YouTube videos and social media content I highlight one college student each week to show off his/her talent with the world. My goal is to give college students a platform
